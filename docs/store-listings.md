### EN

**Oxidatii Privacy Policy**
Last updated: June 25, 2026

**1. Who we are**
Oxidatii (the "app") is operated by the Oxidatii team. Data questions: privacy@oxidatii.life.

**2. Data we collect**
• Account: email, display name, profile picture, birthdate (optional)
• Location: GPS coordinates, only while you use the app or have "live friends" on
• Content: moments, comments, messages, votes
• Technical: device type, OS, app version, IP, error logs
• Payments: handled by Stripe; we do not store card data

**3. Why we collect it**
• Show your friends on the map and show you to them
• Recommend nearby venues
• Send push notifications (invites, messages, votes)
• Detect spam and abuse
• Process Premium payments

**4. Who we share with**
• Supabase (database hosting, EU)
• Cloudflare (edge hosting)
• Stripe (payments)
• MapLibre / OpenFreeMap (map tiles — receive only the visible area, not your identity)
• Apple Push / Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
We do not sell data to third parties.

**5. Retention**
• Active account: as long as you use it
• Messages: until you delete or close your account
• Technical logs: 30 days
• After account closure: full deletion within 30 days

Access, rectification, deletion, portability, objection. Email privacy@oxidatii.life. We reply within 30 days.
